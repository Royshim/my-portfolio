About Me

I’m Roy — 20 years old, Kenyan, and walking a path that wasn’t handed to me. Every line of code I write is a brick I’m laying toward something bigger than myself. I’m a Software Engineering student at Zetech University, a quiet storm with big intent. I believe there’s purpose in the process — that we don’t just move forward in life, we understand it in reverse. This isn’t about proving anything to the world — it’s about building something honest, skillful, and lasting.


I draw strength and clarity from the Stoic minds of Marcus Aurelius, Seneca, and Epictetus. Their words remind me to tame chaos, to remain grounded, and to find direction in stillness. “You could leave life right now. Let that determine what you do and say and think.” Those aren’t just quotes — they’re how I reset, how I calibrate when life feels overwhelming. I’m building in silence, with principles louder than noise.
